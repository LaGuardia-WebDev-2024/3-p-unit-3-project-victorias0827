Step 1: Seperate whites and yolks from the eggs
Step 2: Whisk egg whites until whipped
Step 3: Add 1 1/4 cup of sugar to the egg yolks
Step 4: Add 1/4 cup milk, 2 tsp vannilla, 1/3 cup salted butter, and 2 tbsp of oil into the egg yolks
Step 5: Add 1 1/4 flour, 2 tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt into the egg yolks
Step 6: Fold in/mix egg whites into the egg yolk mixture
Step 7: Grease pan and set oven to 350 degress. Put mix into the pan and leave it in the oven for 20-30 mins
Step 8: Poke holes in the cake once out the oven
Step 9: Pour evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, 1/2 cup milk and 1 tsp of vanilla onto the cake. Let cake sit for around 2 hours
Step 10: In a seperate bowl, mix 2 cups heavy cream, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 tsp vanilla, and a pinch of salt together until it has a whipped cream consistency
Step 11: Put your whipped cream on top of the cake, add cinnamon and strawberries on top (optional). All done!
